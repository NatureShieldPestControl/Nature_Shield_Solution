About Us

Nature Shield Pest Control is a proudly local and eco-conscious business based in Tanauan City, Batangas. We specialize in producing organic pest control solutions that help gardeners and plant lovers protect their crops naturally.

History of the Company

Nature Shield began as a student project under the Bachelor of Science in Entrepreneurship program at Tanauan City College. Inspired by sustainable living, our team experimented with natural ingredients that could combat garden pests without harming plants or the soil. What started as a small experiment eventually turned into a meaningful venture.
